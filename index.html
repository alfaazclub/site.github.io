Meet Alfaaz

Alfaaz is a collection of proud hardworking book worms and shy writers who believe that a pen is indeed mightier than a sword. With big dreams and bigger vocabulary, all the hidden writers and orators have decided to bring their skills finally in the limelight. Therefore ALFAAZ is a dreamland of good literature and poetry which acts under the supervision of ALFAAZ In charge, Mr. P. S. Mann..

 

Student learning in these days is often rote and do little to encourage original thinking.Along with academics, students should participate in activities like public speaking and creative writing to enhance their skills and develop their personality.Students are lacking confidence due to lack of participation in group discussions and the feeling of inferiority among them due to less or no mass contact. Through participation in these gatherings, students will be able to enter a world wholly different from the curriculum typical of era.
